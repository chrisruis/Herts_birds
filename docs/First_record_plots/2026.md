# First spring records in 2026

<img src="_figures/spring_dates_2026.png" width = "750">

This plot shows when 21 of our common spring migrant birds are most likely to first be seen in Hertfordshire and their first dates in 2026 (details of how to interpret the plot below).

We've already seen the first sand martins (a record early bird!) and little ringed plovers back. They will likely be followed by the first wheatears and swallows over the next few days. 

**How to interpret the plot** The vertical line within each box is the average first date for that species. The boxes and whiskers show when we'd expect to first see the species in most years. The small triangles show unusually early or late first records for that species
